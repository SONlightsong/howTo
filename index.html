Album Covers

Canva

Being a songwriter is more than just writing. It's about being an artist. As a songwriter you get to make your own album covers, musics videos, and do your own producing. Canva is a great way to make album covers, becuase there are many free optional templates that you may like.

    You'll Need

  • Computer or Phone
  • Wifi & Internet

    • Steps

    1. Making an album cover on canva is easy. The first thing you need to do is make a free acount, so your projects will be saved.
    2. The next step is create a new project.
    3. Once you have created a new project, then you can search for online templates.
    4. Search for album covers and whatever you theme may be, or what you want on your cover.
    5. After you have selceted your photo, now you can add your title or artist name, and edit any other thing.
    6. Now all you have to do is download your picture and there you have it! Your own album cover.
